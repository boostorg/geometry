DSV (Delimiter Separated Values)

DSV is a text representation of a geometry, explained here: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delimiter-separated_values . DSV can represent a point, a linestring, a polygon, or multi versions of those.

It is currently just a utility in the library, used in samples and tests, to get some textual output of a geometry.
